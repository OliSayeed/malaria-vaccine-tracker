2. ABOUT PANEL — "How the model works"
---------------------------------------
[ ] "This tracker estimates how vaccine deliveries translate into doses
    administered, children fully vaccinated, and the downstream impact on
    malaria cases and deaths. It combines shipment timing, completion rates,
    and efficacy waning to model protection over time."
[ ] "All calculations are client-side and use the data files bundled with this
    site. You can explore assumptions in the sections below and switch
    scenarios using the controls on each view."

[ ] Legend text: "R21 (starts at 75%)" / "RTS,S (starts at 56%)"


4. ABOUT PANEL — "Model assumptions"
-------------------------------------
[ ] "Doses per child: 4 (3 primary doses + 1 booster at 12 months)"
[ ] "Roll-out model: Linear ramp-up over 6 or 12 months (configurable)"
[ ] "Dose timing: Dose 2 at +1 month, dose 3 at +2 months, dose 4 at +14
    months after dose 1"
[ ] "Efficacy curve: Flat at initial level until year 1, then linear decay
    through data points, then exponential extrapolation"
[ ] "3-dose efficacy: Same as 4-dose until year 1, then shifted: E3(t) =
    E4(t+1)"
[ ] "Dose reallocation: Unused doses from children who do not complete the
    course are reassigned over time to vaccinate additional children."
[ ] Age eligibility text (mentions WHO position paper, 5-36 vs 6-60 months)
