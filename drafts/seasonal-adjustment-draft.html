Africa-wide impact — delivered shipments only

Simplified model (no dose cascade, no ramp-up). Shows the seasonal adjustment effect in isolation.

Caveat: Seasonal profiles are country-level averages from literature, not sub-national rollout areas. The adjustment is most meaningful for small, geographically homogeneous countries with high seasonality index R. For large countries (DRC, Nigeria, Ethiopia), sub-national variation would likely dominate. Effects are also small because the 36-month protection window spans many seasonal cycles — timing matters most in the first year when efficacy is highest.
