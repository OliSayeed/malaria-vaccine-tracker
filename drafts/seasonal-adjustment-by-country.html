Model assumptions

  • 4 doses per child; Average completion: 61% reach dose 3, 39.4% reach dose 4
  • 3-dose efficacy curve = 4-dose curve shifted 12 months (no booster)
  • Protection window: 48 months post-dose-3
  • Cases per person per year = country's annual cases ÷ population at risk
  • Seasonal multiplier averaged over 6-month rollout: protection starts months M+2 to M+7
  • Dose-4 timing (month +12 from dose 3) implicit in efficacy curve
  • No dose-dropout cascade reallocation
  • Lives saved = cases averted × country-specific case fatality rate

Methodology

Cases averted per child (flat model). Monthly incidence is assumed uniform at cpp/12 where cpp = annual cases ÷ population at risk. Cases averted:

cases_flat = Σ_{t=0}^{47} e(t) × (cpp/12)  =  cpp × Σe / 12

Cases averted per child (seasonal model). Monthly incidence = cpp × share[m] where share[m] is the fraction of annual cases occurring in month m (sums to 1). With a 6-month linear rollout and 2-month dose-1→dose-3 lag, protection start is averaged uniformly over months M+2 to M+7:

cases_seas = cpp × (1/6) × Σ_{k=0}^{5} Σ_{t=0}^{47} e(t) × share[(M+2+k+t) mod 12]

Why effects are small. The efficacy curve is flat for the first 12 months, during which a full annual cycle of transmission always passes regardless of starting month (shares sum to 1). The timing effect operates only on the declining years 2–4, where the gradient is gentle. This bounds the adjustment to roughly ±R × (efficacy decline range) ≈ ±1% for most countries.

3-dose children. Children completing only 3 doses receive efficacy E3(t) = E4(t+12) — the 4-dose curve shifted 12 months forward, representing the absence of the booster dose.

Completion rates (Average scenario). Dose 2: 73%; Dose 3: 61%; Dose 4: 39.4% of the original cohort.

Lives saved. Applied as lives = cases × (annual_deaths / annual_cases) using the country-specific case fatality rate from the WHO/GBD burden estimates in the dataset.

Excluded countries. DRC, Sierra Leone, Central African Republic, and Guinea-Bissau have shipment records but no matching seasonality profile in the dataset.
