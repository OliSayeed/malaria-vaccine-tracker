Methodology

This tracker estimates vaccine impact using:

  • Waning efficacy – Protection decreases over time per clinical trial data
  • Dose completion – Only 8-71% of children complete all 4 doses
  • Dose reallocation – Unused doses from dropouts go to new children
  • Linear ramp-up – Vaccines administered gradually over 6-12 months
  • 3-dose protection – Children with 3 doses get faster-waning efficacy

Vaccine pricing: R21 at $2.99/dose, RTS,S at $9.81/dose
